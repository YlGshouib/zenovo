How long does it take to build my website? ▼
Most business websites are completed within 5-7 business days. Complex projects with custom features may take 10-14 days. We keep you updated at every stage.
Do I really get a free domain and hosting? ▼
Yes! We include a free .com or .in domain and premium hosting for the first year. After that, you can renew at standard market rates or migrate anywhere you prefer.
Will my website work on mobile phones? ▼
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive, meaning it adapts beautifully to mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops automatically.
Can I update the website myself later? ▼
Yes! We build on user-friendly platforms or provide a simple admin panel. You'll be able to edit text, images, and add new pages without any coding knowledge.
What if I need changes after the website is live? ▼
Your 1-year technical support covers minor text and image updates. For major redesigns or new features, we offer affordable maintenance packages.
How do I connect WhatsApp to my website? ▼
We integrate a floating WhatsApp button that lets visitors message you directly. Just provide your WhatsApp number and we handle the rest — no extra apps needed.
